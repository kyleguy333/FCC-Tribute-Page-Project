Robert McAlpin Williamson

Republic of Texas Supreme Court Justice Robert McAlpin Williamson

Major Achievements

  • 1829- First Prosecuting Attorney for San Felipe de Austin, the Colonial Capital of Texas
  • 1835- First Major of Texas Rangers
  • 1833- Delegate to the Convention of 1833
  • 1835- Delegate to Texas Consultation
  • 1837- Justice of Supreme Court of Texas Republic
  • 1840- House of Representatives of Texas Republic
  • 1843- Senate of Texas Republic
  • 1846- Texas State Senate
